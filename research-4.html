Research

Our work spans the intersection of mathematical optimisation, energy systems engineering, and sustainability science.

The AHMET Research Group develops advanced computational models and decision-support tools to address the complex challenges of the global energy transition.

Research themes
⚗️
Hydrogen Supply Chain Optimisation
Design and planning of cost-optimal hydrogen supply chains from production through storage and distribution to end use, under uncertainty and at multiple scales.

Sustainable Energy Systems Modelling
Integrated modelling of future energy systems incorporating renewable electricity, hydrogen, and carbon capture, with emphasis on sector coupling and flexibility.
Energy systemsSector couplingDecarbonisation

Infrastructure Planning & Investment
Multi-period investment planning for energy infrastructure under uncertainty, including facility location, capacity expansion, and technology selection.

Resilience & Risk Analysis
Quantifying and improving the resilience of energy networks against disruptions, including extreme weather, demand shocks, and infrastructure failures.
